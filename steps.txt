What Data Ingestion Is (Conceptually)

Think of ingestion as a gatekeeper 

Before training starts, ingestion answers:

❓ Where is the data?

❓ Is it accessible?

❓ Has it changed?

❓ Can we copy it safely?

❓ How much data do we have?
